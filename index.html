Nordic leadership & innovation lab

Leadership, AI & RegTech for a disrupted future

Attolloo is a personally driven Nordic lab exploring how technology, regulation and human development can shape better organisations – especially in complex and regulated domains.

Attolloo 2.0 is built gradually alongside a full-time executive role in the Norwegian public sector, with a clear long-term ambition for Attolloo to become the primary arena for this work.

What Attolloo focuses on

Leadership in complex and regulated environments

How to build culture, capacity and execution power when regulation, risk and technology all matter. From silos to collaboration. From compliance-as-burden to compliance-as-capability.

AI, RegTech & market disruption

Artificial intelligence and regulatory change are reshaping entire markets. Attolloo explores how organisations can use AI and RegTech to strengthen insight, control and value creation – not only to “tick the box”.

A curated network of leaders & builders

A small, high-trust network of leaders, founders and ecosystem players working where leadership, technology and regulation meet – with room for honest conversations and shared learning.

Attolloo in brief

Attolloo Group combines advisory work, leadership development and curated arenas such as Attolloo Polaris (Nordic mountain context) and Attolloo Lumina (Southern light and long-term reflection). The common thread is leadership, responsibility, and how technology and regulation shape tomorrow’s organisations.

Built on real-world experience

The work rests on hands-on experience from leading HR, security and privacy in a large public pension organisation – together with years of working with agile teams, product thinking and transformation in both private and public sector.
