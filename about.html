About Attolloo

Attolloo Group is a personally driven Nordic platform for leadership, AI and regulatory transformation. It is not a traditional consultancy, but a long-term lab for exploring how technology, regulation and human development can be combined in practice.

The work spans advisory, leadership development, strategic sparring and carefully curated arenas such as Attolloo Polaris in the Nordic mountains and Attolloo Lumina in a Southern light context.

About Nils Johan Brede

Nils Johan is an executive leader based in Norway, currently combining responsibility for both HR and security & privacy in a major public pension organisation. His background blends organisational development, digital transformation, agile ways of working and leadership in complex, regulated environments.

Attolloo is the next step: a focused space to bring together leadership, AI, RegTech and market disruption in a way that allows for deeper exploration, collaboration and impact over time.

Polaris and Lumina

Attolloo Polaris is inspired by the Nordic mountains – a setting for leadership reflection, resilience and team dynamics.

Attolloo Lumina is inspired by Southern light and long-term perspective – a context for more strategic, future-oriented work on personal and organisational direction.

Both concepts are being developed gradually, with the same foundation: real work, real conversations, and a strong link to everyday leadership challenges in complex organisations.
